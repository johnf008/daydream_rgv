What is Daydream Rio Grande Valley?

Daydream Rio Grande Valley is the first ever game jam for teenagers in the valley, where you can make any game you can think of! Whether you want to make a 2D platformer, a 3D adventure game, or anything that’s been at the back of your mind, Daydream RGV is the place to make it come true!

Our game jam is open to all skill levels! We will have several workshops throughout the 24 hours, and mentors to guide you through your development journey. The only thing you’ll need is the passion to finish your game on time!

Check out a game jam from last year!

#1: Check in & find you team
#2: Attend workshops to learn new skills
#3: Code your game with your team
#4: Submit and demo your game to win prizes
#5: Connect with the Hack Club community!

Who can participate in Daydream?

All high-school & upper-middle-school aged students are welcome to come!

Where will Daydream RGV be held?

Daydream RGV will be held within the Rio Grande Valley. Exact location details will be shared to registered participants

All this, for free?

Yep! Food, swag and good vibes are all included. Plus, if you're joining us from afar, we'll cover the cost of gas or a bus / train ticket.

What do I need?

Your laptop, chargers, toiletries, sleeping bags, and an open mind!

What has Hack Club done before?

Hack Club has run a hackathon in at GitHub HQ, a Game Jam in 50 cities, a hackathon on a train from Vermont to Los Angeles, and more!

I'm not good at coding. Can I still participate?

This game jam is for all skill levels! We'll have workshops and other events so join us and let's learn together.

What if my parents are concerned?

We're here to help! Your parents can reach out to us at daydream@hackclub.com for questions.

What can I make at Daydream?

ANY type of game based on the theme! Platformer, visual novel, clicker game, etc. Be as creative as possible!
